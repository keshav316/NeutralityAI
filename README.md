## Overview

**Neutrality AI** is an AI-powered dispute resolution assistant designed to help financial institutions resolve payment disputes faster, more fairly, and with complete transparency.

Payment disputes and chargebacks often require manual investigation of multiple sources of information, including transaction records, customer complaints, merchant responses, invoices, and policy documents. This process is time-consuming, expensive, and may lead to inconsistent decisions.

Neutrality AI uses **Generative AI, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), and explainable decision-making** to analyze dispute evidence and provide fair resolution recommendations with clear reasoning.

---

# Problem Statement

Millions of payment disputes occur every year, creating challenges for both customers and financial institutions.

Current dispute resolution systems face problems such as:

- Long processing times due to manual investigation
- High operational costs
- Difficulty analyzing large amounts of evidence
- Lack of transparency in decision-making
- Inconsistent dispute outcomes

Customers need faster and fairer resolutions, while financial institutions need scalable systems that improve efficiency without compromising trust.

---

# Proposed Solution

Neutrality AI acts as an **AI decision-support system** that assists dispute investigators by analyzing evidence from both customers and merchants.

The system:

1. Collects dispute-related documents and transaction information
2. Extracts important details using AI
3. Retrieves relevant financial policies and rules
4. Compares customer and merchant evidence
5. Identifies inconsistencies and missing information
6. Generates an explainable resolution recommendation

The goal is not to replace human decision-makers but to provide them with faster and more reliable insights.
